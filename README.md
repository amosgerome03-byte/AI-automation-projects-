# 🤖 AI-Powered Customer Support Workflow

An AI automation case study focused on analyzing a manual customer support process, identifying bottlenecks, and redesigning the workflow using AI-driven solutions.

---

## 📌 Project Goal

Improve support efficiency, reduce response time, and minimize manual workload using AI-powered automation.

---

## 🧠 Key Problems Identified

- Slow ticket assignment due to manual supervisor routing  
- High manual data entry workload for CSRs  
- Slow customer response times  

These issues were discovered by analyzing customer complaints and comparing them with the current workflow.

---

## ⚙️ Proposed AI Solutions

| Problem | AI Solution | Impact |
|--------|------------|--------|
Slow ticket assignment | AI auto-routing | Instant ticket distribution |
High manual workload | AI message analysis + CRM autofill | Reduces repetitive data entry |
Slow response times | AI auto-acknowledgment | Immediate customer confirmation |

---

## 🧩 AI Tools Used in the Design

- AI Message Analysis (Dialogflow / IBM Watson)
- AI Auto-Routing (Salesforce Einstein)
- AI Auto-Acknowledgment (Zendesk triggers)
- AI CRM Autofill (HubSpot AI assistant)
